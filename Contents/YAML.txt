YAML uses simple key-value pair to represent the data. The dictionary is represented in key: value pair.

Example 1:
---
Name: Narasimha
Address: Hyderabad

Example 2: Nested YAML
---
Narasimha:
  place: Hyderabad
  Address: KPHB
Mallikarjun:
  place: Bangalore

Example 3: Multiple values 
---
Fruits:
  - Apple
  - Bananna
  - Mango
Vegerables:
  - tomato
  - potato
